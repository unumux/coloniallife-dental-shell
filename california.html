Life is full of unexpected smiles, and good oral health helps maintain them. Colonial Life dental coverage helps pay for your routine and costly dental care for you and your family, so you can focus on what's important.

The Summary of Dental Benefits and Coverage Disclosure Matrix (SDBC) is provided to help you review proposed policy benefits and provide examples of what you may pay for covered services. The summary is for illustrative purposes only and does not include premium costs. The summary is not intended to be a complete description of the insurance coverage.

For complete details on the covered benefits and limitations, please refer to the Policy. For more information call your Colonial Life benefits counselor or 1-888-400-9304.
